# NextFursa 🧭

A centralized platform designed to bridge the gap between high school students in Jeddah and the impactful extracurricular and training opportunities around them.

## 🔗 Live Platform
You can explore and interact with the platform here: https://nextfursa.netlify.app

## 🎯 Project Overview
Many students miss out on vital development programs simply due to a lack of a centralized information hub. NextFursa was developed as an applied research project to solve this problem, mapping out user workflows and accessible designs tailored to the local student ecosystem.

## 🌟 Core Features Designed
* 🏛️ **Centralized Dashboard:** A curated feed of verified local extracurricular and volunteer opportunities.
* 🔍 **Targeted Filtering:** Sorting features based on interests and application deadlines.
* 🎓 **Student Resource Hub:** A space to help students find training and build their academic profiles.
